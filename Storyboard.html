STORYBOARDS


Storyboards are a crucial part of making a movie, especially if the topic is action and complicated. Storyboards help us visualize scenes, think about the camera angles, and plan ahead.


Another important aspect of storyboards are arrows.
The arrrows determine how the camera is goig to move and
zoom. The arrows inside the box usually how the subject is
moving inside the scene. The arrows outside of the box usually
means how the camera would follow or pan.
